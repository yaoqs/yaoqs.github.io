¶Biography

Yao-Qingsheng received the B.S. degree in Noise and Vibration Control(082502) from Northwestern Polytechnical University (NPU), Xi’an, China, in 2010 and the M.S. degree in Underwater Acoustic Engineering(082403) from Northwestern Polytechnical University (NPU), Xi’an, China, in 2013. He is currently working toward the Ph.D. degree in Electronic Information(085400) with the School of Marine Science and Technology, Northwestern Polytechnical University (NPU), Xi’an, China. His research interests include Underwater Acoustic Engineering, Underwater Acoustic Target Tracking and Information Fusion.
